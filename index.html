About Us

Hi there,

We're two University of Alabama alumni who have a dream to bring craft coffee to Tuscaloosa. We're both Culverhouse graduates. Paul, a finance major. Audrey, a double major in marketing and management. Since college, we've lived in Nashville. Paul serves as the founding math teacher for a charter school. Audrey worked at PR agency and now manages communications for a hospitality startup in Nashville.

We've spent the last couple of years developing the concept. We've put together a team of advisors, we've pursued coffee training, we've saved and we've dreamed.

We can't wait to move back to Tuscaloosa. We'd love to hear from you. Drop us a note at hello@monarchespresso.com.

Cheers,
Paul and Audrey Vermilyea
